The principles of clean software design

S
ingle Responsibily Principle
A function should do one thing and one thing well.
O
pen-Closed Principle
Your modules and classes should be open for extension but closed for modification.
L
iskov Substitution Principle
Any subclass should be interchangeable with and therefore fully compatible with it's superclass.
I
nterface Segregation Principle
Consumers should not be forced to use methods they do not need. Split polluted interfaces into role based interfaces.
D
ependency Inversion Principle
Classes should not rely on other classes, but on interfaces. Invert the dependency to the caller and use dependency injection to pass it to your classes.
